Loan against Long Term Savings Deposit

A member can take this Loan even though the member have a General Loan and any Savings Account Holder can use their own Long Term Savings Deposit amount as surety but don’t take Loan if given Surety.

Features

  • Interest Rate: 2.5% extra than that of the receiving interest from LTSD.
  • Account Holder can take 95% Loan against the deposit amount.
  • Based on Member’s LTSD, any amount upto 95% can be approved as Loan.
  • Loan Tenure: Maximum 36/60 monthly installments
  • Interest Rate is counted on the Basis of Continuous Reducing Manner.
