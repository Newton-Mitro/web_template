Top-Up Loan

Sometimes having a Loan and along with other expenses can make one’s life really difficult. That is why Dhaka Credit’s Top-Up Loan provides the extra financial support during the time of need.

Features

  • Interest Rate: 12% daily basis (Continuous Reducing Method)
  • Along with an existing Loan, an applicant can apply for another Loan by repaying 25% of the previous Loan (General Loan).
  • The 2nd granted Loan will be adjusted with the existing General Loan.
  • If the applicant’s family member has a defaulter, the Loan will not be approved.
  • An applicant can take more than previous General Loan amount based on the capability of the applicant (Share Amount, Surety, Income, etc).
  • Additional Loan can be processed very quickly to meet the applicant’s financial needs.
