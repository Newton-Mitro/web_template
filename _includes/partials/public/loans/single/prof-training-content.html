Professional Training Loan

Every Profession requires some sort of training which can be expensive and it can be an added cost on daily expenses. This Loan is to support those training cost and help the member get through the course without worrying about the High Training Cost.

Features

  • Highest Loan: BDT 100,000 or 20 times the current share.
  • Loan Tenure: After 6 months of completing course, highest 3 years/ 36 equal installments.
  • Interest Rate: 12% (Yearly)
  • Minimum Share Balance: BDT 3000
  • Different Professions require different Educational Qualification & Training.

Requirements

  • Must be a Regular Member of the Union.
  • Education Qualification should be Minimum SSC Pass.
  • Minimum Qualification is Graduate for sales and photography training.
  • Minimum 8th Grade Pass for Junior Nursing, Driving and Catering Training.
