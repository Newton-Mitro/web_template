Higher Education Solvency Loan

This Loan is perfect for students who are looking to pursue Higher Education in abroad for Further studies.

Features

  • Highest Loan: BDT 50,00,000 (50 Lac)
  • Interest Rate: 12%
  • Loan Tenure: 6 Months.

Requirements

  • Monthly Income: (Businessman: BDT 80,000, Service Holder: BDT 50,000)
  • The Parents of the Student(s) must be a regular member of the union.
  • Interest amount should be deposited full at the Savings Accounts before receiving the Loan.
  • Students should apply for the Loan that has to be sanctioned in student’s name. Loan amount will be directly transferred to the student’s bank account until all the processes of Visa is completed. Having Completed student’s bank solvency requirements, the student has to issue a cheque of the similar Loan amount to the Union.
