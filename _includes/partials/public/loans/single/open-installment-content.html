Open Installment Loan

Just like Secure Over Draft (SOD) Loan, a savings depositor can take Loan against Long Term Savings Deposit (LTSD).

Features

  • Interest Rate: 13% Yearly (Continuous Reducing Manner)
  • LTSD depositor can take Loan upto 90% of the total amount of the deposit.
  • Duration of this Loan depends on the fulfillment of the LTSD deposit.
  • LTSD Depositors can take Loan against own LTSD.
  • If LTSD depositor uses LTSD amount for surely of General Loan, Loan cannot be taken against own LTSD.
  • During Office Hour, any amount can be repaid.
  • Every Month, member is bound to pay the interest, if the Loan is not paid.
