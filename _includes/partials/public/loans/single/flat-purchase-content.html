Flat Purchase Loan

Make the dream of owning a Flat in Dhaka come true with the help of this Loan Scheme.

Features

  • Highest Loan Ceiling: BDT (1 Crore 50 Lac)..
  • HDS deposit should be BDT 100,000, for Loan around BDT 20 Lac and BDT 200,000, if the Loan is over BDT 20 Lac.
  • Interest Rate: 13.5% Yearly (Continuous Reducing Method).
  • At the time of flat registration, the flat Loan receiver will provide the power of attorney to the union.
  • At the time of Flat Registration, the union will directly pay the Loan money to the Flat/ Apartment Builder/ Seller.
  • Loan Tenure: 30 Years/ 360 Monthly Installment.
  • Approved Loan shall be repaid according to the monthly installment.
  • Installment plus interest start repayment after one month of sanction.
