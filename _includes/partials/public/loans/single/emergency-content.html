Emergency Loan

Dhaka Credit’s special Emergency Loan (Mother & Child) provides urgent treatment and ensure security during the mother’s pregnancy and child birth.

Features

  • Highest Loan: BDT 200,000.
  • Interest Rate: 12% yearly, (Continuous Reducing Method).
  • Loan Tenure: 3 Yearly/ 36 Equal Monthly installments.
  • Loan Processing Fee: BDT 500 (With Loan Application).
  • Failing to pay Monthly installment, results in fines similar to General Loan.
  • This Loan’s LPS is similar to General Loan.
  • Rebate will not be provided on the Interest of this Loan.
  • With existing General Loan, an applicant can apply for this Emergency Loan.
  • An Applicant can apply for this Loan 3 month after the time of opening Savings & Credit Account.

Requirements

  • Applicant must submit copy of Union’s Digital ID card, Loan Application & recent photo.
  • This Loan will be provided for children = (0-18 month).
  • Applicant must have a Health Care Scheme Account.
  • Applicant has to issue 3 MICR blank cheques with Signature before receiving the Loan.
  • Applicant has to deposit Savings & Share accounts regularly.
