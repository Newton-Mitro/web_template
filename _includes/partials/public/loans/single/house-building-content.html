House Building Loan

This Loan Scheme helps you achieve the dream of owning a House with ease and hassle free.

Features

  • Highest Loan: BDT 50,00,000 or 70% of the Land’s present market value (Not more than BDT 50 Lac).
  • Interest Rate: 13.5% (Yearly) Continuous Reducing Manner.
  • Loan Tenure:30 Years (360 Monthly Installment).
  • Loan Range: (BDT 5,00,000 to 50,00,000)

Requirements

  • Applicant must deposit BDT 100,000 in House Deposit Scheme A/C for BDT 20,00,000 Loan & At least BDT 50,000 in HDS a/c for less than BDT 20,00,000 Loan.
  • Applicant’s Income: (Service Holder: Min BDT 40,000) (Businessman: Min BDT 50,000)
  • 20% of the total Loan as Booking Security from the regular members.
  • MICR Cheque and Land will give Power of Attorney to CCCUL as Surety.
