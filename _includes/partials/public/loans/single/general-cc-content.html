General Credit Ceiling Loan

General Credit Ceiling Loan is created for the development of the memberâ€™s standard of living and provides financial assistance when the necessity arises.

Features

  • Highest Loan: BDT 500,000.
  • Interest Rate: 12% Yearly (Diminishing Manner)
  • Loan Tenure: Highest 3 years/ 36 equal monthly installments.
  • For the security of the Loan money, the applicant requires surety from the members beyond family(s) for 30% surety coverage.
  • Even with existing General Loan, applicant can apply for this Loan.

Requirements

  • Regular applying and repayment of 3 previous Loans is required to qualify for this Loan.
  • Other family members of the applicant (if any in Dhaka Credit) must be regular in all transactions.
  • Must have surplus income for Loan Payment.
  • Applicant must provide BDT 1000 service charge by MICR cheques.
  • If the applicant takes 90% against LTSD, the applicant not required to provide MICR cheques.
  • The applicant must open an LTSD accounts for 10% of the sanctioned loan amount for 3 years on 8% yearly interest.
  • For security reasons, the applicant has to provide 3 MICR blank cheques along with undertaking of family members in non-judicial stamp papers agreeing of Loan payments; and 30% of the applied Loan has to be deposited in the form of LTSD, Share or Savings Accounts.
