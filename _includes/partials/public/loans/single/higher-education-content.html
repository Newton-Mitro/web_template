Higher Education Loan

This Loan Scheme is perfect for students and their parents who wants to support the educational expenses.

Features

  • Member’s share amount should be minimum Tk.3,000 to apply for this Loan.
  • Interest Rate: 12% Yearly.
  • Loan Tenure:3 Years/ 36 equal installments.
  • Applicant’s membership with the Union should be Minimum of 6 Month.
  • Highest BDT 50,000 Loan will be approved for higher studies, technical and professional courses.
  • Repayment starts 6 months after the course is completed.
  • High Priority is given to the potential Good Christian students of BBA, MBA, CA, Hotel Management, Engineering, Medical Studies, BSs Nursing, Technical and Other Professional Courses.

Requirements

  • Students have to achieve at least First Division (A-) in one Exam and Second Division (B) in other examinations including SSC & HSC exams.
