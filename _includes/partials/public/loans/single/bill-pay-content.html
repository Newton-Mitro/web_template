Bill Pay Loan

A typical Christian Businessmen encounters many hurdles to keep its business running such as Cash Constrains. To counter such situations, the union has started “Bill Pay Loan” to support & help in overcoming the financial crisis faced by businessmen.

Features

  • Upto 80% of the bill might be approved.
  • Interest Rate: 13.5% Yearly (Continuous Reducing Manner)
  • Loan Tenure: Highest 60 Days.
  • Contract between the businessman and the union will be signed for a year.
  • Businessman will submit the bills to the third party, the third party will provide the account payee cheque against Bill Receivables to the Union. As soon as the cheque is cashed, the Loan and the interest will be adjusted accordingly and rest of the money will be given to the Loan receiver.

Requirements

  • Business age should be at least 7 years.
  • Applicant must be regular member of the Union.
  • Undertaking must be given.
