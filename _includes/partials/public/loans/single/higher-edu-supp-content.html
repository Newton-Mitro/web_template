Higher Education Support Loan

A member can get higher education in Bangladesh with the support of this Loan without worrying about the cost.

Features

  • Interest Rate: 12% (Yearly), while studying. After completion, pay Principal and interest amount in 60 installment.
  • Highest Loan: BDT 15,00,000 (15 Lac).
  • Repayment after 6 months of completing the course with a Maximum of 5 Years or 60 Monthly Installments.
  • Students studying in Under-Graduate, Post-Graduate or Diploma courses in Different Government affiliated Universities are eligible to apply.
  • Loan is approved quickly without much hassle.

Requirements

  • The applicant must be a member with the union for at least 3 months with BDT 5000 minimum share before applying.
  • The applicant must achieve at least ‘B’ grade / 2nd Division in both SSC & HSC exams.
