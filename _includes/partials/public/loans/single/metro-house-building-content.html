Metropoliton House Building Loan

Anyone can be a proud owner of a High Rise Building in Dhaka with the help of Union’s “Dhaka Metropolitan House Building Loan”.

Features

  • Highest Loan Ceiling: BDT 3,00,00,000 (3 Crore) or upto 70% of the Land’s present market value, whichever is less.
  • Interest Rate: 13.5% Yearly (Continuous Reducing Method).
  • Loan Duration: 30 Years/ 360 Monthly Installments.
  • During Construction, installment payment is not necessary but interest should be paid.
  • Applicant can repay Loan from other sources to reduce the amount of the loan.
  • Once the building construction is complete, a union representative will be deployed to rent the flats and collect the rents to repay the installments and interest of the Loan.
  • If any disparity to the Loan and Interest, calculate using the formula: (Total Collected Rent – Interest = Installment).

Requirements

  • Must be a regular member of the Union.
  • Age: (Min 25 – Max 55).
  • Monthly Income: Service Holder( BDT 40,000), Businessman( BDT 50,000).
  • Must be able to finance the Foundation Structure (Including great Beam) to Quality.
  • After Dhaka Credit releases the Loan in series of Installments, Calculated interest payment must be made from next month based on the Loan Amount.
