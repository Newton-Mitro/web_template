Double Deposit Scheme

This Deposit Scheme is very Lucrative that provides double benefits to the depositors within a certain period of time.

Features

  • Any Amount more than BDT: 10,000/- can be placed as double deposit.
  • Interest Rate: 12.5% per annum.
  • Tenure: 8 years or 96 Months.
  • After 2 years, 90% of the deposited amount can be taken as Loan.
  • Instant Loan with Surety for Self Loan or Family Members
  • In case of emergency, any amount can be withdrawn.
