Bee Savers

This account is specially designed for small children to develop and encourage savings habit and to make them understand & interested in the activities of Co-operative Credit Union.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: 50/- per month.
  • Interest Rate: 6% per annum.
  • NO yearly fee charges
  • After 1 Year, 50% of account balance can be withdrawn for educational purposes.
  • BDT 500/- is given as a reward on opening Bee Savers account within 60th day of the birthday.
  • Children up to the age of 12 year old will be termed as “Bee Savers”.
  • Bee Savers are Auto-updated to Smart Savers at the age of 13 years.
  • Bee Savers are given a “Piggy Box” to inspire saving habit at home.
  • Kids are encouraged to make their own transactions.
  • Chocolates are given as rewards for making the transaction.
  • Bee Savers Day is celebrated every year with inspirational gifts to encourage them for good results in their School.

Requirements

  • To open STD account: 2 stamp size color photos, Baptism/ Birth Certificate/ NID/ Birth registration photocopy.
  • Any bank cheque/pay order has to be in the name of The Christian Co-Operative Credit Union Ltd. Dhaka.
