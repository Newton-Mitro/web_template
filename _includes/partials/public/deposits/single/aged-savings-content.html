Aged Savings

Aged Savings scheme is specially designed to ensure financial security at the time of retirement.

Features

  • Monthly Deposit: BDT (200, 300, 500, 700, 1000, 1500 & 2000).
  • Interest Rate: 9% per annum
  • Flexible Tenure: (10, 15, 20, 25 or 30 Years)
  • After Completing 2 Years, 90% of the deposit amount can be received as Loan; in case of Emergency, deposit can be withdrawn at anytime as premature encashment.
  • Any Savings Account holder can open one or multiple accounts under this scheme.
