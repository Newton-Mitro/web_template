Housing Deposit Scheme (HDS)

Dhaka Credit’s Housing Deposit Scheme (HDS) was introduced to help the member’s accumulate funds for making their Dream of owning a House come true.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: 50/- per month.
  • Interest Rate: 5% (Per Annum, Rate Fixed according to the market) .
  • HDS can be used as a multiplier of Loan.
  • Can be used as Surety for self & family member’s Loan.

Requirements

  • Completed Account opening form.
  • Passport Size Color Photograph.
  • Proof of Identification such as Passport, Driving License or Voter’s ID.
