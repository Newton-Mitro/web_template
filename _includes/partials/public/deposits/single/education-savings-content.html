Education Saving Account

This Deposit scheme is mainly focused on providing savings for educational expenses for children studying in Home or Abroad.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: Monthly- (200, 300, 500, 1000, 2000 or 3000).
  • Interest Rate: 9% per annum.
  • Duration: Years (5, 10, 15 and 20)
  • After 1 year, 80% of total deposit can be withdrawn for education purpose and 90% of total deposit can be taken as Loan without interest.
  • After 5 years or completing HSC, double amount of self-deposit can be received as loan @ 9% Interest per annum.
  • Help bearing child’s education cost and create scope for higher education.
  • Instant loan for any Emergency situations.
  • Anyone can open this a/c from the age of (1 month – 18 years).
