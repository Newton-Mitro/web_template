STD Account

Savings is an important aspect of life for each and everyone and one can never fully prepare for the financial requirement that may arises for any situation.

Features

  • Minimum Account Opening Deposit: Tk 500/-
  • Interest Rate: 2.5%, twice a year on minimum existing balance.
  • Minimum Account Opening Deposit: Tk 2000/-
  • Interest won’t be provided if balance is less then BDT 2000/-.
  • Account statement will be sent to account holder’s address every 3 months.
  • If any inconsistency is detected, it could be rectified with appropriate evidence with 7 working days.
  • Prior notice for withdrawal is not required for amount BDT 5,00,000/-, but require (1 Working day) prior with written notice to withdraw more than BDT 5,00,000/-.

Requirements

  • To open STD account: 2 stamp size color photos, Baptism/ Birth Certificate/ NID/ Birth registration photocopy.
  • Any bank cheque/pay order has to be in the name of The Christian Co-Operative Credit Union Ltd. Dhaka.
