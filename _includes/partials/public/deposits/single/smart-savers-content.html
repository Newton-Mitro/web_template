Smart Savers

This account is designed to encourage savings habit among the Teenagers and to make them interested in becoming an active and integral part of Co-operative Credit Union movement.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: 100/- per month.
  • Interest Rate: 6% per annum.
  • No yearly fee charges
  • 50% of account balance can be withdrawn for educational purposes.
  • 25% discount on enrollment in Spoken English, Gym and/or IELTS course.
  • Bee Savers will be auto updated to Smart Savers from the age of 13.
  • Children from the age of 12 year & 1 day to 18 year can open this account.
  • Chocolates are given as rewards for making the transaction.
  • Smart Savers are auto transferred to full membership account at the age of 18.
  • Smart Card is also available for Smart Savers.
