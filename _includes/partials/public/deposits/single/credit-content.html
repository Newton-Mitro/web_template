Credit Account

Dhaka Credit’s share account has been specially designed to cater the individuals who want to be self reliant with all the facilities that Dhaka Credit has to offer for its valuable members.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: 10/- per month.
  • Account can be opened with a minimum amount of tk.330/-.
  • Attractive Dividend is given according to the decision made in the Annual General Meeting (AGM).
  • Members can stand for guarantee (Surety) for self and/or another member’s loan.
  • Members are entitled to apply for various loans.

Requirements

  • 2 Stamp size & 1 Passport Size color photo, Baptism Certificate and Photocopy of Government Birth Certificate or NID..
  • Christian individuals of 18 years of age, living in Dhaka, Gazipur, Narayanganj & Munshiganj district are eligible for account.
