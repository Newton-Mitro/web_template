Festival Savings account

Festival Savings account is designed to give members the opportunity to increase their money for various expenses during the festive season.

Features

  • Minimum Deposit: BDT 300/- Per month.
  • Interest Rate: 7% per annum.
  • Any amount less than BDT 5000/- can be deposited per day during office hour.
  • Any amount can be withdrawn 15 days before Easter Sunday and Christmas.
