Fr. Charles J. Young

Founder of Dhaka Credit

The pioneer of the Credit Union Movement in Bangladesh, Fr. Charles J. Young CSC was born in May 3rd 1904, in New York, USA. His full name is Fr. Charles Joseph Young CSC. He was the founder of The Christian Co-Operative Credit Union Ltd. Dhaka, the largest Credit Union in Bangladesh and South Asia. Fr.
